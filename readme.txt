== Installation ==
1.	Install the plugin either by uploading it via FTP or by using the 'Add Plugin' function of WordPress.
2.	Activate the plugin at the 'Plugins' tab.
3.	Visit https://app.headstore.com/#signup and chose a registration method.
4.	You are now on the HEADSTORE portal, here you can setup your experts.
5.	Connect your account with the payment provider Stripe under 'My Account' to accept payments generated through calls. 
6.	Click on the grey expert-banner under 'My Experts'. Set your rates under 'Settings' and define your availability under 'Availability rules'. Now you're ready to use these profiles on your Wordpress site.
7.	Connect your HEADSTORE account with your profiles on Wordpress under 'Headstore'. These profiles can be used directly in the WYSIWYG-Editor or under 'Appearance > Widgets'.

It's recommended to save the settings after every little change so the interface can update.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
**How can I create a HEADSTORE account? (step by step)**
1.	When you visit https://app.headstore.com/#signup chose one possible registration method. If you chose the Email signup, fill in all your personal information. Only your expert company, first and last name will be displayed on your 'Call-Me Button'. Additionally you can write anything (e.g. your position as 'Consulting' or 'Author' or a request like 'Call me' or 'Ask me anything' etc.) on the 'Call Button text' field.
2.	After you successfully approved your email address, you can login to your account. First navigate to 'My Account' and connect your account to Stripe. This is necessary to accept payments generated through calls. Answer a few questions about your you, your products and your payment information.
3.	Next click on the grey expert-banner under 'My Experts'. Set your rates and change the profile image under 'Settings' and define your availability under 'Availability rules'. Now you're ready to use this profile on your Wordpress site. You can create multiple experts on the HEADSTORE website.
4.	You can now connect your Headstore account on Wordpress under 'Headstore'. Your account then can be used directly in the WYSIWYG-Editor or under 'Appearance > Widgets'. All the experts associated with the account can be accessed in this way.

**What is Stripe?**
Stripe is our online payment processor. It's an well established company that allows both private individuals and businesses to accept payments over the Internet. If you would like more information about Stripe such as how secure it is or the type of protection it gives you please visit https://support.stripe.com/

**How can I test my 'Call-Me Button'?**
On the HEADSTORE website, you have to navigate to the menu 'My Experts'. After clicking on the grey banner you select 'Embed Call-Button'. Then click on your 'Call-Me Button' and a new window will open, showing only your 'Call-Me Button'. Copy Paste the link into another browser and call yourself. If you select to make a paid call, you will have to enter your creditcard data; after the call you can refund the money.

**What can I do if my 'Call-Me Button' is displayed in grey?**
First ensure that you are either logged in to your HEADSTORE account (on app.headstore.com) with your computer. If you are not logged in, your 'Call-Me Button' will be displayed in grey as you to let users know that you are not available. Please check that the expert you would like to use is also online and that there are no availibiliy rules which automatically set the expert as being offline.
In general save the settings after every change so the interface can update.

**What can I do if the shortcode appears on my website instead of a 'Call-Me Button'?**
If this occurs it is probably due to the account that the shortcode is linked to has been deleted from your accounts page. 

**Can I change my rates?**
You can change them as often as you want, unless you are in the middle of a HEADSTORE-call. You can do this under 'My Experts < Settings'.

**What payment options do you support?**

Australian, Canadian and European businesses can accept Visa, MasterCard and AmericanExpress.
For more details check: https://support.stripe.com/questions/which-cards-and-payment-types-can-i-accept-with-stripe

**Can I use a HEADSTORE 'Call-Me Button' when the website does not use Wordpress?**
Of course! You can easily embed the javascript of any 'Call-Me Button'. When you login on the HEADSTORE website, go to 'My Experts' tab and click on the grey expert-banner. Then navigate to 'Embed Call-Button' and follow the 3 steps. Copy the section of code which is displayed and paste it where you want it in the html-file of your website.
